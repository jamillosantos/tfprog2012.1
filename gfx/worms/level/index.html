Terrain images

These are images from each terrain style. There are a few images which all terrains have:
  • debris.gif is the background animation
  • back.gif is the horizon background image
  • soil.gif is what the soil "underneath" the terrain looks like (ie what you see when near the edges of a hole).
  • text.gif is what the main texture of the terrain looks like
  • grass.gif shows the grass - the left hand side is the top, the right hand side is "grass" at the bottom of an overhang

Important note

All these images are ©Team17 Software Limited - please respect their rights.
